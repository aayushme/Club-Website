We are enthusiast and passionate

Programers, Developers & Designers

We believe in learning more and more, practical implementation and sharing our knowledge. We host and attend short sessions, workshops, hackathons, programing events, gaming events, virtual reality events and much more.

We are the offical programing club of YMCA University Of Science And Technology, Faridabad, India. We were sponsored by companies like codeshala, hackerearth, codechef, hackerrank for conducting coding contests and were are sponsored by companies like digitalocean, dot tech, jetbrains, mozilla, codeshala, sapphire engine, sticker mule for conducting our annual hackathon 'Zenith'.

We conduct a test called 'Manthan' to enroll new coders and developers to our club every year. We have been conducting various series of coding contests such as 'Code For YMCA' which focuses on promoting participation towards ICPC, 'Code Cadet' which focuses on promoting coding culture in the newbies to coding, 'Code Relay' which focuses on improving data structures topic wise. We host variety of coding, gaming and fun events during our annual college fest 'Culmyca 2017'. We develop prefest online games every year such as 'Xunbao', 'Hackon'.

We are winners of several international hackathons such as 'Angel Hacks', 'Code for india' and many more. We have participated in ACM ICPC at regional level and India finals. We have developed lots of projects as well.
